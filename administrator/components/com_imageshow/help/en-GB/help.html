We are committed to providing you the best possible support for all our products. There 2 levels of support we provide.

    Dedicated Ticket Support is for developers who bought Developer License of our products. With ticket support we can investigate issue in depth and provide solution much faster. Guarantee 12 hours response time. One more great benefit here is that we provide support for all questions related not only to the product, but also to your Joomla! configuration.
